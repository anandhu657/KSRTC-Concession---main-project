The Kerala Urban Road Transport Corporation is a state-run bus company which operates more than 500 low-floor buses in Kerala.
KURTC runs complete and individual services in Ernakulam & Thiruvananthapuram districts.
 The remaining 12 districts are divided into 5 clusters;
Cluster I comprises Kozhikode, Wayanad and Malappuram;
Cluster II Kottayam, Thodupuzha, and Pathanamthitta;
Cluster III Kannur and Kasaragod;
Cluster IV Thrissur and Palakkad;
and Cluster V Kollam and Alappuzha.
